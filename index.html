About Me

Hi, I am Zhen Li (李振), a Ph.D. student at Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Shixia Liu and Prof. Hui Zhang.

My research primarily focuses on the intersections between visual analysis and machine learning. Specifically, my work spans two complementary directions:

VIS4AI: I develop visual analytics methods to explain and diagnose machine learning models with different architectures and their training data biases. Through interactive visualizations, I enable the effective identification and mitigation of both model and data issues, thereby enhancing model performance and fairness.

AI4VIS: I develop large-scale datasets and leverage generative models to advance the automatic generation of infographic charts, which combine visual elements (such as charts and images) with textual information. My research focuses on bridging the gap between simple charts and complex, human-designed infographics, enabling large vision-language models (LVLMs) to automatically create and understand infographic charts.
